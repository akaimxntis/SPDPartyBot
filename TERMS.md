Last updated: 2026-05-08

## 1. Purpose of the bot

SPD Party Bot provides tools for creating and managing party invites inside Discord servers, including:

- Party Hub messages
- Party creation forms
- Join/Maybe/Not going buttons
- Party slots and queues
- Party management tools
- Server-specific configuration
- Logs, reminders and automatic party closing
